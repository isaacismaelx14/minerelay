# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to providing a respectful, harassment-free environment for all
participants.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, patient, and constructive.
- Assume good intent and discuss technical disagreements with evidence.
- Accept feedback and iterate professionally.
- Focus on what is best for the project and community.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful language.
- Personal attacks, trolling, or intimidation.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Any conduct that creates an unsafe environment.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, pull
requests, or other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

Maintainers may also apply temporary or permanent bans for repeated or severe
violations.

## Reporting

Report incidents by opening a private communication channel with maintainers via
GitHub. Include links, screenshots, timestamps, and context.

All reports will be handled as confidentially as possible.
